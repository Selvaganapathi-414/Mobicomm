Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



How can I recharge my prepaid mobile online?
You can recharge your prepaid mobile by visiting a recharge website or using a mobile app. Simply enter your mobile number, select your operator, choose a plan, and proceed with payment.
What payment methods are accepted for prepaid recharges?
Most platforms accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, wallets, and sometimes even cash on delivery in select cases.
How do I check my prepaid balance and validity?
You can check your balance and validity by dialing USSD codes provided by your operator or using the telecom provider’s official app.
What should I do if my recharge fails but the amount is deducted?
If your recharge fails but money is deducted, the amount is usually refunded within 2-3 working days. If not, contact customer support with your transaction ID.
Can I recharge for someone else?
Yes, you can recharge for any number by entering their mobile number and choosing the appropriate plan.
Are there any offers or cashback available on prepaid recharges?
Yes, many platforms offer cashback, discounts, and promo codes on prepaid recharges. Check the offers section of the recharge platform.
Can I recharge my prepaid number in advance?
Yes, most operators allow queueing of recharges, meaning your next plan will be activated once the current plan expires.
